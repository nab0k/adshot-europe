Guinness:
Lucky You

For St. Patrick's Day, we designed a lucky merch line for a Guinness giveaway. Green, stylish, with character — for real fans of the Irish stout.

Year
2025
Type
Design
Designed the full St. Patrick's merch line — from scratch cards to prizes handed out at Guinness bars.
The gift set included tote bags, socks, coasters, keychains, and badges — all in a recognizable identity inspired by the festive St. Patrick's vibe.

We also went to the party ourselves — tested the mechanic, watched the guests' reactions, and caught that same festive atmosphere with Guinness.
