Behind the process

The creative and technical challenges we solved

01

Depicting a morgue without AI censorship bans. AI tools have strict content filters — we had to find workarounds in visual style and framing to show the subject matter without triggering them.

02

Generating complex objects and scenes correctly. Hands, surgical tools, specific lighting — each required iterative prompting and creative problem-solving to achieve consistent results.

03

Ukrainian lip-sync. AI lip-sync tools are trained predominantly on English. Achieving accurate Ukrainian language lip movement required experimental approaches and editing workarounds.
