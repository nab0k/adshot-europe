Grimbergen:
Belgian Legend

Bridging nearly a thousand years of history with the present day — a campaign for one of Belgium's oldest beers.

Year
2024
Type
Campaign
Burned, but never destroyed.
Grimbergen Abbey was founded in 1128. Over the centuries it was burned to the ground three times — and rebuilt every time. The Phoenix symbol isn't just branding. It's history.

Legendary, but contemporary.

We connected the brand's rich history with its modern-day relevance by blending visual styles in both the key visual and the film.

The story of Grimbergen was told through the beer itself — watch how beautifully it plays in the light.
