Process

No endless back-and-forths, no scope creep surprises. A clear 5-step process from first call to final file — and we hit the deadline.

01

Discovery call (30 min)

We learn your objective, audience, and existing assets. A proposal arrives within 48 hours — scope and price, no fluff.

02

Brief & concept

We write the creative brief and present two concept directions. You choose one. One decision, one direction forward.

Production

Script, visuals, voiceover, motion — all in-house at our Lumiere studio. You see a rough cut on day 7.
